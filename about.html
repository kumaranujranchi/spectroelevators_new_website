About Spectro Elevators

A decade of innovation, excellence, and unwavering commitment to vertical transportation safety.

Our Story

A decade of innovation, safety and excellence.

Founded in 2014 by engineer Anubhav Singh in Patna, our company began with a clear vision: to revolutionize vertical transportation through innovative design and uncompromising quality. From our very first passenger elevator installation in a modest office building, we have steadily grown into a trusted provider of reliable elevator solutions across India.

In just a decade, we have successfully delivered 100+ projects, serving diverse needs from residential apartments to commercial spaces and institutional facilities. Our commitment to engineering precision, safety, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of property developers, facility managers, and architects.

Today, Spectro Elevators continues to move forward with a dedicated team of 10+ professionals, blending craftsmanship with modern technology to create elevator systems that are not only functional but also enhance the built environment with elegance and efficiency.

We continuously invest in research and development to pioneer new technologies and design solutions. Our engineering team holds multiple patents and actively collaborates with architects to push the boundaries of what is possible.
