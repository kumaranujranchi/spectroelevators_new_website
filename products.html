Elevator Types & Tech Specs

Comprehensive guide to capacities, speeds, mechanics, and safety features for all major vertical mobility divisions.

Category 01

Passenger Elevator Systems

Engineered with high-efficiency gearless permanent magnet motors and smart digital microprocessors, our passenger lifts provide smooth and rapid transit within multi-family apartments and premium corporate centers.

  • Capacity Range: 4 to 20 Persons (300kg - 1360kg)
  • Rated Speed: 1.0 m/s to 2.0 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Geared Traction / VVVF Gearless
  • Key Safety: ARD (Auto Rescue Device) + Overload Sensor
  • Primary Uses: Apartments, Offices, Hotels
  • Controller: 32-bit Microprocessor Dual Loop

Category 02

Hospital & Stretcher Lifts

Designed to meet the rigorous demands of healthcare facilities, these elevators feature extra cabin depth and wide telescopic doors to comfortably slide stretchers, medical carts, patient beds, and support technicians.

  • Capacity Range: 15 to 26 Persons (1025kg - 1768kg)
  • Rated Speed: 0.75 m/s to 1.5 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Gearless AC VVVF Drive
  • Key Safety: Door Hold Button + Emergency Backup
  • Primary Uses: Hospitals, Clinics, Nursing Homes
  • Open Style: Center Opening Wide Doors
Inquire About Hospital Lift
Category 03

Goods & Freight Elevators

Our goods lifts are constructed with structural steel framing and checker-plate floors, engineered to bear heavy, dynamic loads. They are highly resilient and suitable for factories, warehouses, and storage hubs.

  • Capacity Range: 1000 kg to 5000 kg
  • Rated Speed: 0.25 m/s to 0.75 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Heavy Geared Traction / Hydraulic
  • Key Safety: Collapsible Gate Switch + Safety Buffer
  • Primary Uses: Factories, Storage Centers, Malls
  • Cabin Finish: Anti-slip Checker-plate Steel
Inquire About Goods Lift
Category 04

Machine Room-Less (MRL) Lifts

By placing the compact gearless traction machine inside the lift shaft itself, we eliminate the need for a separate rooftop machine room. This cuts structural civil work and provides high energy-efficiency.

  • Capacity Range: 4 to 15 Persons (300kg - 1025kg)
  • Rated Speed: 1.0 m/s to 1.75 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Gearless Synchronous PMSM Motor
  • Key Safety: VVVF Smart Brake + Governor Release
  • Primary Uses: Modern Residential Flats, Offices
  • Energy Saving: Up to 40% less power consumption
Inquire About MRL Lift
Category 05

Home Lifts & Villa Elevators

Tailor-made for luxury duplexes, bungalows, and penthouses. These lifts require no deep shaft pit (just 150mm pit or ramp) and can easily operate on single-phase domestic electrical connections.

  • Capacity Range: 3 to 6 Persons (250kg - 450kg)
  • Rated Speed: 0.3 m/s to 0.5 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Hydraulic / Gearless Traction MRL
  • Key Safety: Emergency Battery Lowering + Phone Line
  • Primary Uses: Villas, Rowhouses, Penthouse Duplexes
  • Electricity: 230V Single-Phase Standard Supply
Inquire About Home Lift
Category 06

Scenic & Capsule Lifts

Enhance the visual aesthetics of your building with panoramic capsule lifts. Fitted with safety glass panels, custom circular shapes, and elegant lighting, these are popular choices for high-end shopping centers and hotels.

  • Capacity Range: 6 to 15 Persons (408kg - 1025kg)
  • Rated Speed: 1.0 m/s to 1.5 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Gearless VVVF / Hydraulic
  • Key Safety: Tempered Laminated Safety Glass
  • Primary Uses: Malls, Showrooms, Star Hotels
  • Cabin Options: Semi-circular, Hexagonal, Rectangular
Inquire About Capsule Lift
Category 07

Hydraulic Elevator Systems

Utilizing high-pressure hydraulic fluid pistons, these systems provide exceptional load-carrying capacities with smooth starts and landings. Best suited for low-rise commercial sites and home applications.

  • Capacity Range: 3 to 12 Persons (250kg - 816kg)
  • Rated Speed: 0.3 m/s to 0.6 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Italian Hydraulic Pump Block
  • Key Safety: Burst Valve Protection + Hand Pump Manual
  • Primary Uses: Low-Rise Buildings, Luxury Duplexes
  • Civil Advantage: No overhead machine room required

Category 08

Automobile & Car Elevators

Specially reinforced cabins built to move cars and utility vehicles between basement parking lots and multi-level showrooms. Equipped with dual control panels so drivers can operate it without stepping out.

  • Capacity Range: 3000 kg to 5000 kg
  • Rated Speed: 0.25 m/s to 0.5 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Heavy Dual Hydraulic Pistons
  • Key Safety: Photo-electric Position + Safety Buffer
  • Primary Uses: Multi-level Garages, Auto Showrooms
  • Control: Dual Operator Panels (left & right)

Category 09

Service Lifts & Dumbwaiters

Designed for quick and hygienic transfer of food, laundry, medical supplies, and general documents across floors. Ideal for hotels, luxury residences, restaurants, and medical centers to optimize staff operations.

  • Capacity Range: 50 kg to 250 kg
  • Rated Speed: 0.25 m/s to 0.4 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Compact Traction / Drum Type
  • Key Safety: Electro-mechanical Door Interlocks
  • Primary Uses: Restaurants, Hotels, Hospitals, Pantry Rooms
  • Structure: Medical-grade Stainless Steel (SUS 304)

Category 10

Outdoor & Exterior Lifts

Custom-built to run along exterior walls, providing architectural beauty and panoramic views while saving valuable indoor space. Features full weatherproof engineering to withstand external heat, rain, and dust.

  • Capacity Range: 4 to 12 Persons (300kg - 816kg)
  • Rated Speed: 0.5 m/s to 1.0 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Hydraulic / MRL Traction
  • Key Safety: IP54 Waterproofing + Emergency Lowering
  • Primary Uses: Commercial Facades, Scenic Villas, Glass Towers
  • Finish: Anti-corrosive Powder Coating / Stainless Steel

Category 11

Stair Lifts & Chair Lifts

Safe, reliable, and compact mobility solution for elderly or physically challenged family members. Installs directly onto existing staircase steps without structural changes to the home.

  • Capacity Range: 120 kg to 160 kg
  • Rated Speed: 0.15 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Rack & Pinion Battery-Powered
  • Key Safety: Seatbelt + Safety Obstruction Edge Sensors
  • Primary Uses: Duplex Staircases, Nursing Homes, Clinics
  • Rail Configuration: Straight or Curved Custom Tracks

Category 12

Pneumatic Vacuum Lifts

Futuristic, air-driven circular elevators operating via vacuum pressure technology. Requires zero pit, hoistway, or machine room, making them the ultimate stylish addition for pre-existing luxury duplexes, penthouses, and homes.

  • Capacity Range: 1 to 3 Persons (150kg - 240kg)
  • Rated Speed: 0.15 m/s to 0.2 m/s
  • Drive Technology: Pneumatic / Air Pressure Vacuum
  • Key Safety: Mechanical Fall-Arrest Anchors + Battery Backup
  • Primary Uses: Penthouses, Duplex Villas, Space-Restricted Homes
  • Cabin Finish: 360-Degree Panoramic Polycarbonate Glass
