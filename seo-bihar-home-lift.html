Villa Elevator Systems

Compact, Pit-less, and Single-Phase Lifts for Bihar Homes

Residential bungalows require compact systems that do not alter the building's structural integrity. At Spectro Elevators, we import high-quality Italian hydraulic pistons and gearless MRL traction kits that fit alongside existing staircases.

Our home lifts require a pit depth of only 150mm (about 6 inches) or can be accessed via a low floor ramp. They are engineered to operate on standard 220V single-phase electrical meters, consuming as much power as a standard 1.5-ton split air conditioner.

No overhead machine room needed
Pit-less or low 150mm pit designs
Single-phase domestic electricity
Automatic battery lowering (ARD) built-in
