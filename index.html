FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about installing and maintaining elevators in Bihar.

For modern home lifts, specifically Machine Room-Less (MRL) and hydraulic lifts, minimal civil work is needed. In many cases, we do not require a deep pit (only 150mm to 300mm is sufficient) or an overhead machine room. They can be installed inside a self-supporting steel structure alongside your staircase.
An Auto Rescue Device (ARD) is a critical safety system. If utility power goes out while the lift is running, the ARD automatically switches on using an emergency battery pack. It runs the elevator cabin slowly to the nearest landing floor, opens the automatic doors, and safely evacuates the passengers before turning off.
We offer two main packages:
  • Semi-Comprehensive AMC: Includes monthly maintenance checks, oil and grease servicing, and emergency breakdown support. Parts are charged extra.
  • Comprehensive AMC: Covers monthly servicing, breakdown calls, plus all major mechanical and electronic parts (like control cards, motors, ropes, and locks) replaced free of charge.
Yes! Our home lifts are engineered with high-efficiency gearless permanent magnet synchronous motors (PMSM) or hydraulic power packs that can operate on standard 220V single-phase domestic power supply. They require very little energy, similar to a 1.5-ton household air conditioner.
